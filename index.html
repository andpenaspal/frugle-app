What is Frugle?

Frugle is a smart shopping companion designed to help you spend less without thinking harder. By connecting meals, products, and real prices, Frugle turns everyday grocery shopping into informed decisions. Over time, it helps you build better habits, reduce waste, and take control of your food budget — one shop at a time.

How it works

  • Add your products: Include the items you buy regularly and your favorite ingredients.
  • Save your recipes: Create recipes with ingredients and quantities for easy planning.
  • Plan your meals: Build meal plans for the week or month using your recipes.
  • Scan & track prices: Log prices by scanning barcodes to build your personal price database across supermarkets.
  • Generate smart shopping lists: Frugle picks the cheapest options and groups items by store so you always know where to buy and save.
